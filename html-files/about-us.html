About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Kyle Bourke, Pav's Roofing Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional roofing services with integrity, quality, and attention to detail that homeowners and businesses deserve.

Despite being relatively new to the industry, our team brings together decades of combined experience in residential and commercial roofing. We've quickly established ourselves as a trusted name in the region by focusing on what matters most - delivering outstanding results and customer satisfaction.

What started as a small operation has grown into a full-service roofing company, serving both residential and commercial clients across the region. Our growth is a testament to our commitment to excellence and the trust our clients place in us.
