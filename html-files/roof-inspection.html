Expert Roof Inspections for Peace of Mind

Regular roof inspections are essential for maintaining your property's integrity and avoiding unexpected repair costs. At Pav's Roofing Solutions, our certified roof inspection specialists conduct thorough assessments that identify potential problems early, helping you make informed decisions about maintenance and repairs.

Whether you're buying a new property, preparing for storm season, or simply want to ensure your roof is in optimal condition, our detailed inspection services provide the comprehensive analysis you need to protect your investment.

Our Roof Inspection Services Include:

  • Pre-Purchase Inspections: Comprehensive assessments for property buyers to identify potential roofing issues before purchase.
  • Storm Damage Assessments: Post-storm evaluations to document damage for insurance claims and repair planning.
  • Annual Maintenance Inspections: Regular check-ups to catch minor issues before they become major problems.
  • Insurance Inspections: Professional reports required by insurance companies for policy renewals or claims.
  • Warranty Inspections: Detailed assessments to verify roof condition for warranty compliance and claims.
  • Leak Detection Inspections: Specialized investigations to locate the source of water penetration and moisture issues.
  • Safety Compliance Inspections: Evaluations to ensure your roof meets current building codes and safety standards.

What We Inspect

Our comprehensive roof inspections cover every aspect of your roofing system to ensure nothing is overlooked:

Roofing Materials

Detailed examination of tiles, shingles, or metal roofing for cracks, missing pieces, corrosion, and general wear.

  • Tile integrity and alignment
  • Metal sheet condition and fasteners
  • Shingle damage and granule loss

Flashing & Sealing

Critical waterproofing elements around chimneys, vents, skylights, and roof penetrations.

  • Flashing condition and seal integrity
  • Chimney and vent flashing
  • Skylight sealing and weatherproofing

Gutters & Drainage

Assessment of gutter systems, downpipes, and roof drainage to ensure proper water management.

  • Gutter condition and alignment
  • Downpipe functionality
  • Leaf guard and debris buildup

Structural Elements

Evaluation of roof structure, support systems, and overall structural integrity.

  • Rafter and beam condition
  • Sagging or structural deformation
  • Load-bearing capacity assessment

Our Roof Inspection Process

  1. Initial Consultation: We discuss your specific concerns, inspection requirements, and schedule a convenient time.
  2. Exterior Assessment: Comprehensive examination of all visible roofing components from ground level and roof access points.
  3. Interior Inspection: Checking roof spaces, ceilings, and walls for signs of water damage or structural issues.
  4. Documentation: Detailed photography and notes of all findings, both positive and concerning areas.
  5. Report Preparation: Compilation of findings into a comprehensive written report with photos and recommendations.
  6. Report Delivery: Presentation of findings with explanation of issues and recommended action priorities.
  7. Follow-up Support: Ongoing consultation for repair planning and maintenance scheduling based on inspection findings.
When Should You Get a Roof Inspection?

Consider scheduling a professional roof inspection:

  • Before purchasing a property
  • After severe weather events (storms, hail, high winds)
  • Annually as part of preventive maintenance
  • When you notice leaks or water stains
  • Before making insurance claims
  • When your roof is approaching 10-15 years old
