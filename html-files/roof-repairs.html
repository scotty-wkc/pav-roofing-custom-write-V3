Expert Roof Repair Solutions

At Pav's Roofing Solutions, we understand that a damaged roof requires prompt attention to prevent further issues. Our comprehensive roof repair services address everything from minor leaks to major storm damage, restoring your roof's integrity and protecting your property.

With our team of experienced roofing professionals, we identify the root cause of roofing problems and implement lasting solutions that extend your roof's lifespan while maintaining your home's safety and appearance.

Common Roof Issues We Repair:

  • Leaking Roofs: We identify and fix the source of leaks to prevent water damage to your home's interior and structure.
  • Storm Damage: Quick response and repairs after hail, wind, or falling debris damage your roofing system.
  • Broken or Missing Tiles/Shingles: Replacement of damaged or missing roofing materials to maintain proper water shedding.
  • Flashing Issues: Repair or replacement of damaged flashing around chimneys, skylights, and other roof penetrations.
  • Gutter Problems: Fixing sagging, damaged, or clogged guttering systems that can lead to roof and foundation issues.
  • Ridge Capping Repairs: Addressing loose, cracked, or missing ridge caps that protect vulnerable roof junctions.
  • Roof Pointing: Repairing deteriorated mortar between tiles to prevent water infiltration and tile movement.
  • Valley Issues: Repairing damaged valleys where water concentrates during rainfall.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common signs that your roof needs repairs include water stains on ceilings or walls, missing or damaged shingles/tiles, granules in gutters, sagging areas, visible light through the roof boards, increased energy bills, and mold or moss growth. If your roof is over 15 years old or you've experienced severe weather events, it's wise to have a professional inspection even without obvious signs of damage.

For emergency situations such as storm damage or significant leaks, we typically respond within 24 hours. Our team will prioritize your case based on severity and implement temporary solutions if needed until permanent repairs can be completed. We understand that roof emergencies can't wait and strive to provide the fastest possible service to prevent further damage to your home.

Many homeowner's insurance policies cover roof repairs when damage is caused by unexpected events like storms, falling trees, or fire. However, damage resulting from lack of maintenance or normal wear and tear is typically not covered. We can assist you with documenting damage for insurance claims and work directly with your insurance company when appropriate to make the process smoother.

Minor repairs can often be completed in a single day, while more extensive damage might require 2-3 days. Factors affecting the timeline include the extent of damage, roof type, weather conditions, and material availability. After our inspection, we'll provide you with a specific timeframe for your particular repair needs. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
