Comprehensive Residential Roofing Solutions

At Pav's Roofing Solutions, we understand that your home's roof is both a critical protective element and a significant aesthetic feature. Our residential roofing services combine premium materials, expert craftsmanship, and attention to detail to deliver roofing solutions that protect your home and enhance its curb appeal.

Whether you're building a new home, replacing an aging roof, or repairing damage from storms or wear and tear, our experienced team is equipped to handle projects of all sizes with precision and care.

Our Residential Roofing Services Include:

  • New Roof Installation: Complete installation of quality roofing systems for new constructions or full replacements.
  • Roof Replacement: Expert removal of old roofing and installation of new, modern systems.
  • Roof Repairs: Fixing leaks, damaged tiles, worn flashing, and other issues that compromise your roof's integrity.
  • Roof Restoration: Revitalizing aging roofs through cleaning, repairs, and protective coatings.
  • Roof Inspections: Comprehensive assessments to identify potential issues before they cause damage.
  • Gutter Installation: Complete gutter systems that effectively channel water away from your home.
  • Skylight Installation: Adding skylights to bring natural light into your home while ensuring watertight integration.

Residential Roofing Materials

We work with a variety of high-quality roofing materials to meet your specific needs, preferences, and budget:

Concrete Tiles

Durable, versatile, and available in various profiles and colors. Concrete tiles offer excellent protection and a classic look.

  • 50+ year lifespan
  • Fire resistant
  • Low maintenance

Terracotta Tiles

Premium clay tiles known for their distinctive appearance and exceptional longevity. Perfect for Mediterranean and heritage designs.

  • 70+ year lifespan
  • Naturally beautiful color that doesn't fade
  • Superior thermal properties

Colorbond® Metal Roofing

Modern, lightweight steel roofing with excellent durability and energy efficiency. Available in a wide range of colors.

  • 40+ year lifespan
  • Lightweight and strong
  • Energy efficient with thermal reflection

Asphalt Shingles

Cost-effective and versatile with a variety of styles and colors. Popular for their ease of installation and repair.

  • 20-30 year lifespan
  • Economical choice
  • Wide range of design options
