Project Overview

Complete gutter replacement for a coastal home in Bondi. The homeowners wanted to upgrade from their old sectional gutters to a premium seamless system with integrated leaf guards for minimal maintenance and superior water management in the coastal environment.

Services Provided

  • Complete removal of old sectional gutters
  • Custom seamless gutter fabrication
  • Professional installation with proper fall
  • Integrated leaf guard system
  • Downpipe optimization and replacement

The Challenge

The homeowners contacted us with several issues plaguing their 15-year-old sectional gutter system:

  • Frequent leaks at the joints causing water damage to fascia boards
  • Constant blockages from falling leaves and debris
  • Poor water flow due to inadequate fall and undersized downpipes
  • Corrosion and rust staining on the coastal-facing sections
  • Time-consuming maintenance required multiple times per year

Living close to the ocean, the property faced additional challenges from salt air exposure and frequent storms, making a robust, low-maintenance solution essential.

Our Solution

After assessing the property's unique coastal requirements, we designed a comprehensive gutter solution:

  1. Complete System Removal: Safe removal of all existing sectional gutters, brackets, and deteriorated downpipes.
  2. Custom Seamless Fabrication: On-site fabrication of continuous aluminum gutters to eliminate leak-prone joints.
  3. Optimal Sizing and Fall: Calculation and installation of properly sized gutters with correct fall for maximum water flow.
  4. Integrated Leaf Guards: Installation of premium mesh leaf guards to prevent debris accumulation while maintaining water flow.
  5. Enhanced Downpipe System: Upgraded to larger diameter downpipes with additional outlets for improved drainage capacity.
  6. Corrosion Protection: Use of marine-grade aluminum with protective coating suitable for coastal environments.

The Execution

The project was completed efficiently over two days:

Day 1: Removal and Preparation

We began by carefully removing the existing gutter system, protecting the landscaping and identifying any fascia board damage that needed attention. Our team then measured and calculated the precise fall requirements for optimal water flow, taking into account the property's unique roofline and drainage needs.

Day 2: Fabrication and Installation

Using our mobile gutter machine, we fabricated seamless aluminum gutters on-site to exact specifications. The new system was installed with heavy-duty brackets spaced appropriately for coastal wind loads. Integrated leaf guards were fitted during installation, and all downpipes were replaced with larger diameter options positioned for maximum efficiency.

Quality control checks ensured proper water flow, secure mounting, and complete integration with the existing roof drainage system.

The Results

The seamless gutter system delivered outstanding performance:

  • Complete elimination of leaks with no joints to fail over time
  • Dramatically reduced maintenance requirements due to leaf guard system
  • Superior water handling capacity even during heavy coastal storms
  • Enhanced curb appeal with clean, professional appearance
  • Corrosion-resistant marine-grade materials for long-term durability
  • Estimated 25+ year lifespan with minimal upkeep required
