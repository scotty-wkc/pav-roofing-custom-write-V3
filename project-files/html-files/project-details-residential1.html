The Challenge

When the homeowners first contacted us, they were experiencing multiple issues with their existing 25-year-old concrete tile roof:

  • Frequent leaks during heavy rain, particularly around the chimney and valley areas
  • Cracked and broken tiles throughout the roof surface
  • Poor insulation leading to higher energy costs
  • Dated appearance that detracted from the home's modern architecture

Additionally, the homeowners wanted the work completed within a tight timeframe of one week to minimize disruption to their daily lives.

Our Solution

After a thorough assessment, we proposed a comprehensive roof replacement plan:

  1. Complete Removal: Safe removal and disposal of the old roofing materials, including all tiles, underlayment, and deteriorated flashing.
  2. Structural Repairs: Replacement of damaged battens and repair of compromised roof decking discovered during the tear-off.
  3. Premium Materials: Installation of high-quality concrete tiles in a modern slate profile that complemented the home's architectural style.
  4. Enhanced Waterproofing: Application of premium synthetic underlayment and proper installation of new valley and chimney flashing.
  5. Ventilation Upgrade: Integration of improved ridge venting to enhance attic airflow and reduce energy costs.

Our team worked efficiently, creating a detailed schedule that allowed us to complete the project within the required timeframe while maintaining our quality standards.

The Execution

The project was executed in several key phases:

Day 1-2: Preparation and Demolition

We began by preparing the site, protecting landscaping and property with tarps and shields. Our team then carefully removed the old roofing materials, uncovering and documenting structural issues that needed attention.

Day 3: Repairs and Preparation

Once the old roof was removed, we repaired the identified structural issues, replaced the damaged battens, and installed the new synthetic underlayment with proper overlaps and sealing at all penetrations.

Day 4-5: Installation and Finishing

The new concrete tiles were installed using proper techniques to ensure longevity and performance. Special attention was given to valleys, ridges, and the area around the chimney to prevent future leaks. We completed the project with proper ridge capping and installation of the ventilation system.

Throughout the entire process, our team maintained constant communication with the homeowners, providing daily updates and addressing any questions or concerns promptly.

The Results

The completed roof replacement delivered impressive results:
