The Challenge

The homeowners in Cronulla contacted us after experiencing recurring leaks that had persisted for over two years despite previous repair attempts:

  • Water stains appearing on ceilings in multiple rooms during heavy rain
  • Mold growth in the corner of the master bedroom
  • Previous repair attempts that only provided temporary solutions
  • Coastal environment accelerating deterioration of roofing materials
  • Urgency to resolve issues before the upcoming storm season

The challenge was to identify all leak sources and provide a comprehensive solution that would withstand the harsh coastal conditions while being cost-effective compared to full roof replacement.

Our Solution

After conducting a thorough inspection using advanced leak detection methods, we developed a targeted repair strategy:

  1. Comprehensive Assessment: Used water testing and thermal imaging to identify all leak sources, including hidden damage not visible during previous inspections.
  2. Strategic Flashing Replacement: Complete replacement of deteriorated flashing around the chimney, valleys, and roof penetrations with marine-grade materials suitable for coastal conditions.
  3. Tile Repositioning: Careful removal and repositioning of tiles that had shifted due to coastal winds, ensuring proper overlap and water shedding.
  4. Enhanced Waterproofing: Application of specialized sealants and membrane systems in critical areas to provide additional protection against driven rain.
  5. Preventive Measures: Installation of additional clips and fasteners to prevent future tile movement in high-wind conditions.

Our approach focused on addressing root causes rather than symptoms, ensuring a long-lasting solution for this coastal property.

The Execution

The leak resolution project was completed efficiently over two days:

Day 1: Assessment and Primary Repairs

We began with a comprehensive roof inspection, documenting all issues and testing for leaks using controlled water application. The most critical areas around the chimney and main valley were addressed first, with complete flashing replacement and sealing.

Day 2: Secondary Repairs and Testing

On the second day, we completed tile repositioning, applied specialized coastal-grade sealants, and conducted extensive water testing to ensure all leak sources had been eliminated. Final quality checks included inspection of all repair areas and cleanup.

Throughout the process, we kept the homeowners informed of our findings and progress, providing photographic documentation of issues discovered and repairs completed.

The Results

The targeted leak resolution delivered excellent results:

  • Complete elimination of all water infiltration issues
  • Successful testing through several heavy rain events
  • Prevention of further mold growth and interior damage
  • Enhanced roof resilience against coastal weather conditions
  • Cost savings of approximately 75% compared to full roof replacement
  • Peace of mind for homeowners during storm season

The repairs have now withstood multiple storm events without any recurrence of leaks. The homeowners were particularly impressed with our diagnostic approach and the thoroughness of our solution.
